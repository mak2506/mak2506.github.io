About Fantasy Foods


Since 2000

Fantasy Foods is founded in 2020 in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh,India. The journey begins with only 2 members and now Fantasy Foods are serving the Indian dishes all over the country with a huge and big service support staff and valuable customers who dedicated and motivate us to serve them with happy hearts. This year Fantasy Foods is celebrating their 1st anniversary with a family of 1500+ members and a nationwide love among customers.

Our Menu


Samosa

Freshly cooked aloo samosa, all time favorite. Stuffing contains aloo(potatoes), peas, paneer(indian cheese), indian masalas.

Chola Bhatura

One of the most demanding recipe of ours, Chola Bhatura is the key to reach people. Contains a chatpata chana masala curry and a white-flour made Bhatura. You get onion rings and pickle as supplement.

Idli Shambhar

The all way South Indian classic, all time favorite for morning breakfasts.

Gulab Jamun

As Indians we love Gulab Jamun more than anything as in sweets. A yummy mawa made fried ball, completely dipped in sweet sugar syrup. Each bite is a treat to heart.

Sahi Paneer

The gravy you love more than any non-veg food, this adds a flovourful boomb of taste, loved by everyone. This curry is loved by all and along with Butter Naan this is amazing.
